About Us

Welcome to Rafiza’s Cake Shop! Based in Dallas, Texas, Rafiza’s Cake Shop is a home-based bakery specializing in tiered cakes and Desi sweets for all occasions. We proudly offer halal friendly, gluten-free, and other dietary-conscious options upon request.

At Rafiza’s Cake Shop, every dessert is made meticulously and creatively, with the design process done collaboratively with each customer to ensure their vision comes to life.
